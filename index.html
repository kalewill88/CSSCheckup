Traveler's Guide

How to travel 101

It is very important to plan your travels well ahead of time. This prevents issues arising on the road. The following are questions to ask yourself, then plan before traveling:

  • Where do you want to go?
  • How many stops will you make?
  • How much time do you have? At each stop?
  • Figure our how you plan to travel. (ie. plane, car, train or boat)
  • Where do you want to stay during your trip?
  • Where to eat?
  • Should I take the scenic route?
  • Places I want to stop along the way?
